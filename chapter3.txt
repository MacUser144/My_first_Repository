The hours passed in a blur, the ticking of the clock on the wall the only sound in the room. Mary had tried to distract herself with a book, but the words blurred together. She couldn’t focus. Instead, she kept thinking about the uncertainty that loomed ahead. What was she going to do? Where would she find another job in a town like this? The weight of it all settled heavily on her chest, making it hard to breathe. Eventually, she stood up and walked to the window, looking out at the now-quiet streets. A sense of emptiness washed over her, but somewhere in the back of her mind, a spark of determination flickered. It wasn’t over yet. She wasn’t done fighting.
